Lamborghini's current lineup includes a range of high-performance supercars and hypercars. These models include: Lamborghini Aventador, Lamborghini Huracán, Lamborghini Urus, and Lamborghini Sián. For the latest model releases, please visit the official Lamborghini website or consult your nearest Lamborghini dealership.

Lamborghini vehicles are luxury performance cars, and prices can vary significantly depending on the model and customization options. Approximate base prices are the following:
  • Lamborghini Aventador: Starting from approximately $500,000 USD.
  • Lamborghini Huracán: Starting from around $230,000 USD.
  • Lamborghini Urus: Starting around $225,000 USD.
  • Lamborghini Sián: Pricing starts at approximately $3.6 million USD, reflecting its status as a limited-production hypercar.

Yes. Lamborghini offers a wide range of customization options for nearly every model. Customers can select from a variety of exterior paints, interior materials, trim finishes, and performance upgrades. You can personalize your vehicle with options such as custom colors, stitching, carbon fiber elements, and more. Discuss your preferences with your Lamborghini dealer to create a truly unique vehicle.

Lamborghini vehicles are renowned for their speed and performance. Some key speed figures include:
  • Lamborghini Aventador: Top speed of approximately 217 mph (350 km/h).
  • Lamborghini Huracán: Top speed of around 202 mph (325 km/h).
  • Lamborghini Urus: Top speed of 190 mph (305 km/h), making it one of the fastest luxury SUVs in the world.
  • Lamborghini Sián: Top speed of 217 mph (350 km/h), thanks to its hybrid powertrain.
